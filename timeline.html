April 8, 2016

New Direction for App!

During our meeting with Mingru, we discussed some of the serious limitations we realized our current project would have. We realized that we did not have enough time to make the app meaningful and cover all of the corner cases for all of the majors (we spent way too many hours debating one requirement while trying to figure out the ELE major). We wanted to make a meaningful app in this class, and focus on coding rather than studying all of the Princeton degree requirements. Therefore, we all decided (along with Mingru), to change the vision of our app. Our app will generally stay the same, but instead be marketed only for COS majors. By focusing only on one major, we will now have the time to finally focus on the front-end, and will be able to add interesting features (study abroad feature, AP credit feature, schedule sharing, certificate feature...also hoping to have an AI-esque component where it suggests what classes you can/should take next and/or what certificate you might want to do based on the classes you've taken!)
